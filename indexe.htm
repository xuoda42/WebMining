Shirko Alexander Nikolaevich

Master’s student at Kuban State University, field 01.04.02 “Applied Mathematics and Informatics”

A goal-oriented student with deep knowledge of web technologies, data analysis, and programming. I have experience in backend development and business process integration. I am responsible, organized, and highly motivated for professional growth.

Autobiography

Personal Development
I was born in 2002 in Krasnodar. From an early age, I showed a keen interest in computer technology and programming. My parents always supported my interests, and close friends and mentors contributed to my curiosity and desire for self-development.

In childhood, I was fond of competitive programming and solving complex mathematical problems, which developed analytical thinking and perseverance. I showed particular aptitude for logic and technical creativity. From a young age, I dreamed of creating my own projects and developing software. My hobbies included computer games, studying algorithms, and experimenting with web technologies.

The most vivid memories are of school programming competitions and successes in Olympiads. Among my achievements are high results in computer science contests and participation in academic projects. The school I attended provided a strong academic foundation, and my teacher, Vladimir Borisovich Sukhov, had a significant influence on my professional and personal development.

Professional Development
My professional preferences began to take shape in school through participation in Olympiads and project work. A crucial role was played by my parents and mentors, who supported my enthusiasm for technology and programming. The choice of Kuban State University to study applied mathematics and informatics was motivated by my interest in analytical disciplines and the promising IT field.

During my university studies, I delved deeply into programming, web technologies, and data analysis. Courses in algorithms, databases, and web development were especially useful, and my professors provided essential knowledge and practical skills. In 2024, I entered the master’s program to continue professional development and study modern technologies in IT.

I currently work in an implementation department in the telecommunications field, where I am involved in business processes and programming. My professional achievements include participation in projects focused on automation and IT systems integration. My ongoing interest in technology and data analysis continues to support my professional growth.

Goals, Plans, and Vision of the Future
In the coming years, I plan to deepen my professional knowledge and skills, complete my master’s degree, and participate in large-scale IT projects. In the long term, I aim to develop my career in programming and project management, as well as to create my own innovative projects.

I am considering various ways to achieve these goals, including professional development, participation in research projects, and collaboration with IT companies. I see my future as a combination of continuous growth, professional achievement, and opportunities for personal development. I believe that the IT field will continue to evolve dynamically, offering new opportunities for specialists in programming and business technology integration.
