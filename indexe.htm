Makhalin Semyon Aleksandrovich

Master's student at KubSU (Kuban State University), program 01.04.02 “Applied Mathematics and Computer Science”

A student with deep knowledge of web technologies, data analysis, and programming. I have experience in backend development and working with business processes.

Autobiography

Personal development
I was born in 2002 in the city of Budyonnovsk. From early childhood I showed a keen interest in computer technologies and programming. My parents always supported my interests, and close friends and mentors helped shape my curiosity and drive for self-development.

As a child I was passionate about competitive programming and solving challenging math problems, which developed my analytical thinking and perseverance. I showed particular talent in logic and technical creativity. From an early age I dreamed of creating my own projects and developing software. My interests included computer games, studying algorithms, and experimenting with web technologies.

My brightest memories are school programming competitions and successes at olympiads. Among my achievements are high results in computer science contests and participation in educational projects. The school where I studied provided a strong academic foundation, and its principal had a significant influence on my professional and personal development.

Professional development
The formation of my professional preferences began back in school through participation in olympiads and project work. My parents and mentors played an important role by supporting my interest in technology and programming. I chose Kuban State University to study applied mathematics and computer science due to my interest in analytical disciplines and the prospects of the IT industry.

During my university studies I studied programming, web technologies, and data analysis in depth. Courses in algorithms, databases, and web development were especially useful, and my professors provided the knowledge and skills needed for practical work. In 2024 I entered a master's program to continue professional development and study modern IT technologies.

I currently work in a development department in the MedTech sector, where I am immersed in business processes and programming. Key professional achievements include participating in the development of integrations for medical information systems (MIS), as well as designing the architecture for new tasks. My interests in technology and programming continue to contribute to my professional growth.

Goals, plans, and vision for the future
In the coming years I plan to deepen my professional knowledge and skills, complete my master's studies, and take part in large IT projects. In the long term I aspire to develop a career in programming and project management, as well as to create my own innovative projects.

I consider different ways to achieve these goals, including professional development, participation in research projects, and collaboration with IT companies. I see my future as a combination of continuous growth, reaching professional heights, and opportunities for personal development. I believe the IT field will continue to evolve dynamically, opening new prospects for specialists in programming and the business integration of technologies.
